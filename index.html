📖 About (200+ words):

Md. Sabbir Hosen is a passionate computer science educator with a strong foundation in modern software engineering. Graduating with a BSc in CSE from PUST, he specializes in bridging the gap between theoretical concepts and real-world applications. With over 4 years of teaching experience at the diploma level, Sabbir believes in project-based learning and encourages students to build innovative solutions. His technical stack includes Java, Python, JavaScript, and cloud basics. He has guided multiple student teams to win national tech competitions, including the "BTEB Innovation Award 2024". Sabbir actively contributes to the departmental curriculum design, integrating AI awareness and cybersecurity fundamentals into the CST syllabus. His research interests include web accessibility and educational technology. Outside the classroom, he runs the "CodeHub" programming club, mentors final-year projects, and organizes hackathons. Sabbir's teaching philosophy revolves around curiosity, ethics, and lifelong learning. He regularly updates his skills through MOOCs and industry certifications. Students admire his approachable mentoring style and clarity in explaining complex algorithms. He also promotes collaborative coding and open-source contributions among diploma learners. His goal is to produce skilled graduates who can thrive in the IT industry or pursue higher studies abroad. With a blend of academic rigor and practical insights, Md. Sabbir Hosen continues to shape the future technologists of Thakurgaon Polytechnic Institute.
